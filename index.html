JOE

JOE stands for Journey Of Enigma, it is a big competetion which brings students from differents clubs and universities. It takes place in 3 days where challenges about computer science in general will be launched between the differents condidates.The idea of this event is to guide the condidates to the right path throught puzzles that they must solve in a limited time by respecting well defined rules. This event has several objectives : learning, skills developpement and training with the spirit of fun and joy.

There will be 12 teams where each one is made of 8 members, and it will be preferable to have people with diverse skills since the challenges will relate to several fields.The challenges are 5 with different degree of dificulties and durations , obviously an X challenges is more difficult than Y .for qualifying to the next challenges the teams must gain a certain number of points. The last challenge is the biggest because it is about creating a real life project like a web application thing that requires a lot of work and time , this challenge will be between the last four teams wich will succeed in reaching the final phase of competition.
